1. Influencer Marketing Data Analysis

Type: Academic Project (Data Analysis for Business Module)

Role: Data Analyst

Problem: A consumer electronics brand needed a cost-effective influencer strategy that balanced reach and engagement.

Approach & Outcome: I analysed the top 100 UK YouTubers using Excel (pivot tables, engagement ratios) to identify trends and proposed a tiered influencer marketing strategy. This balanced high-reach influencers with mid-tier creators who had higher engagement rates, improving campaign ROI. The project demonstrated how data analysis can inform business strategy.

3. Student Well-being and Comfort Food Analysis

Type: Academic Project (Business Intelligence in Practice Module)

Role: Data Analyst & Team Collaborator

Problem: The project aimed to analyse the relationship between comfort food consumption, student stress levels, and academic performance to better understand student well-being.

Approach & Outcome: I used Python in Google Colab to clean, analyse, and visualise survey data collected from students. Through correlation analysis and visual storytelling, we identified that balanced diets and mindfulness activities improved academic performance, while heavy reliance on fast food and caffeine during stress correlated negatively with grades. The findings were presented with actionable recommendations for improving student health and learning outcomes.
