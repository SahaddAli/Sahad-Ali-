Restaurant Digital Transformation

This project demonstrates how a small restaurant can modernise its communication and customer engagement through a simple, cost-effective digital platform. As the Chief Technology Officer (CTO) for this initiative, I designed a responsive web solution that streamlines customer interaction and booking processes.

Business Context

The restaurant aimed to improve customer engagement, simplify booking requests, and enhance its online presence. The goal was to design a lightweight website that connects customers directly to the business through email and social media, while providing a professional and user-friendly experience.
