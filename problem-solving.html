Business Context

As CTO for Delight Dine, the goal is to modernise how the restaurant engages customers: improving online visibility, simplifying bookings, and creating reliable digital communication with minimal operational overhead.

Design Choices — Quick Explanation

The page is intentionally simple and mobile-first: clear contact details and social links at the top, followed by a short form. This reduces friction for mobile users who want to book quickly. The form fields were chosen to balance business needs (date, party size, accessibility notes) and user friction (keeping required fields minimal).
